Fina Ahadi

Writer & Poet, including the award-winning Born on a Height

Graduating from Parsons School in 2015, Fina Ahadi relocated to Barcelona to work on a series of novels and poetry books. She recently completed her Masters at the University of Berlin and is working on an afro sci-fi interpretation of the End of the World.
